Rock Kickass

Rock Kickass is an 8-bit, platformer that we built for the Github Game-off. After the competition ends we're planning to develop more enemies, levels and powerups and then release the game on Android/iPhone and Windows8.

Until then, you can play the current version here.

Final Frontier: First Contact

In the future, earths first contact with alien life won't go exactly as we expected. Joe Storm was just your average member of the 14th earth space marine detachment assigned as a security force for an earth outpost.

One day the outpost is attacked by a previously unknown spaceship. During a counter-attack, Joes ship is surrounded by a blue light and disappears. Now, he must face wave after wave of never-before-seen enemy forces so that he can become the victor of the vicious alien "sport" that he has been entered into.
